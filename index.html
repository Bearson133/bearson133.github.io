Hi, I'm Josh, A Brooklyn based Front End Developer. This page is a reflection of my skills and will constantly change to reflect what I've learned.

When COVID-19 turned society upside down, I was one of the many people whose life had changed. Isolating was not in my plans to start the decade, but the additional time has allowed me to finally apply myself to my interest in programming and problem solving. While difficult, I find coding to be extremely fun and satisfying. Turning a blank page into a representation of your imagination and skill is awesome to me. I'm on a journey to turn my new skills into a satisfying career. I've used FreeCodeCamp and CS50 along with various other resources to learn.

When I'm not in front of the screen, I love cooking, learning, reading, photography, exploring NYC, being outdoors at the park and beach, and watching movies. I love living in Brooklyn and meeting all of the interesting people.

I'm really glad you took the time to check out my site. I'll update it regularly and hopefully you get a little enjoyment from what you see. The current version is purely HTML and CSS. If you would like to contact me to give feedback, collaborate, or just say hey, my contact info is down below!

I am currently not taking clients for freelance work at the moment, but that will change soon!
